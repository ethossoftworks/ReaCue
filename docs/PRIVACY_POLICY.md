# Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 14, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application ReaCue (hereafter referred to as "the Application") for
Android, iOS, and macOS devices, which was created by Ryan Mitchener and Ethos Softworks.
### 1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The Application is built with a strict user-privacy 
philosophy.
* No Personal Data: We do not ask for, collect, or require names, email addresses, phone numbers, accounts, or any 
  other personally identifiable information.
* No User Tracking: We do not track your location, individual usage habits, or cross-app online activity.
* No Cloud Storage: Any user data or configurations you create within the Application remain strictly on your local 
  device and are never transmitted to our servers.

The only data automatically processed is anonymized, technical crash data used solely to keep the Application stable 
and bug-free, as detailed in Section 3 below.

### 2. Device Permissions
The Application may request certain device permissions (such as access to bluetooth, location, or microphone) 
solely to enable specific features on your device.
   * These processes happen entirely locally on your Android, iOS, or macOS device.
   * We have no access to the files, images, or functionalities utilizing these permissions, and no data from them is 
     ever transmitted.

### 3. Automatic Crash Reporting and Diagnostics
To help us identify and fix software bugs, performance issues, and application crashes, the Application utilizes 
automatic crash reporting services provided by our store platforms (such as Google Play Console, Apple App Store 
Connect, and/or Firebase Crashlytics). When the Application crashes or encounters a critical error, a diagnostic report 
is automatically generated and sent to us. This report contains strictly non-personally identifiable technical 
information, including:
* The type of device used (e.g., iPhone 15, Google Pixel 9, MacBook Air).
* The operating system version (e.g., iOS 19, Android 16, macOS 16).
* The state of the Application at the time of the crash (the "stack trace").
This information is used exclusively to improve the stability and performance of the Application. It cannot be used to
identify you or trace back to your specific device.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
Because the Application does not collect any personal information from any user, we do not knowingly or unknowingly 
collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or any other age group).

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective 
date. Since we do not collect email addresses or personal contact info, we recommend reviewing this page periodically 
for any updates.
